# Code of Conduct

## Our standards
Contributors are expected to:
- be respectful and professional
- focus on constructive technical feedback
- avoid harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks

## Unacceptable behavior
- harassment or intimidation
- hateful or discriminatory language
- deliberate disruption of collaboration
- publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement
Repository maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block contributors for violations.

## Reporting
Report conduct issues privately through maintainer channels listed in `SUPPORT.md`.
